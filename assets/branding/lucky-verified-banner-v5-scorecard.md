# Lucky Verified Banner v5 Scorecard

## Scope

- Direction: `premium_editorial`
- Headline: `Lucky is now a` / `Verified Discord Bot`
- Variants:
    - `assets/branding/lucky-verified-banner-v5a.png`
    - `assets/branding/lucky-verified-banner-v5b.png`
- Baseline reference:
    - `assets/branding/lucky-verified-banner-v4.png`

## Decision Notes

- `v4` remains readable, but the composition feels generic and template-like.
- `v5` introduces asymmetric zones, compact Discord-style check chip, textured mesh, and single-mascot identity.
- Tweak pass applied:
    - Smaller headline sizes
    - Strict left-edge alignment between both headline lines
    - Discord logo inside the verification chip
    - Increased mascot-to-wordmark spacing in the header row

## Evaluation Matrix (1-5)

| Criteria                   | v5a | v5b | Notes                                                                    |
| -------------------------- | --- | --- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Editorial hierarchy        | 4   | 5   | v5b has cleaner left-text rhythm and stronger asymmetry.                 |
| Spacing rhythm             | 4   | 5   | v5b keeps wider breathing room between identity row and statement block. |
| Visual texture quality     | 4   | 5   | v5b mesh layers are less flat while staying low-noise.                   |
| Verification chip subtlety | 5   | 5   | Both chips are compact and visually secondary to headline.               |
| Brand fit and readability  | 5   | 5   | Both preserve Lucky tone and remain clear at reduced scale.              |

## Winner

- Selected: `assets/branding/lucky-verified-banner-v5b.png`
- Promoted to: `assets/branding/lucky-verified-banner-v5.png`

## Validation

- Dimensions: `1200x675` PNG
- Clipping: no clipping for wordmark/headline/chip at 100% and 50%
- Mascot usage: single mascot only
- Verification mark: Discord logo + compact check chip (`Discord logo + Verified + check`)
